About Jaffeiz

Our Journey Since 2010

Founded in the heart of the city in 2010 by visionary chef Haider Jaffri, Jaffeiz started as a modest family kitchen with a dream to share the rich, aromatic flavors of authentic Pakistani cuisine with the world. Over the years, what began with just five tables has blossomed into an internationally celebrated dining destination.

From our signature Biryani — slow-cooked to perfection with fragrant basmati rice and tender meat — to our velvety Korma, sizzling Karahi, and the most comforting cup of Chai this side of the Indus, every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and relentless pursuit of excellence.

Today, Jaffeiz boasts a team of 50+ dedicated chefs and staff, serves over 100 affordable meals, and delivers worldwide. We've been featured in Food & Travel Magazine, Michelin Guide, and have been awarded "Best Pakistani Restaurant" five years running. Our secret? Love. Love for food, love for our guests, and love for the culture we represent.

"Food is our common ground, a universal experience." — Haider Jaffri
